==========================================
NOTES ON OUTPUT FORMAT
==========================================

1. All outputs should be clear and concise
2. Numbers should be spoken naturally ("1 of 4" not "one of four")
3. Star ratings use both symbols and spoken text for clarity
4. Context is always provided (screen name, item count, position)
5. Help text includes all available commands
6. Confirmations are always provided for actions
7. Errors are descriptive and suggest solutions
8. Navigation feedback is immediate
9. Stats are grouped logically for easy comprehension
10. Proper punctuation for natural speech pacing
